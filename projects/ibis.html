Ibis Academy

Early Partnership

A Foundation of Growth

Ibis Academy is one of the leading educational institutions and has been a cornerstone of our journey since the very early stages of Epoq Zero. Our relationship is built on a shared commitment to digital excellence in the education sector. Over the years, we have collaborated on a vast array of projects, ranging from small utility tools to massive enterprise systems.

Our work with Ibis has touched almost every aspect of their operations. We've built robust billing systems, specialized student enrollment platforms, and comprehensive accounting and reporting modules. We also implemented a small-scale CRM to manage their initial leads, which eventually paved the way for more complex integrations.

Digital Transformation

Comprehensive Ecosystem

The collaboration further expanded into the "Study Abroad" domain, where we developed specialized tools to manage international student journeys. Our design team also revamped their website and app UI/UX to ensure a modern, seamless experience for their students and staff alike.

Today, Ibis Academy utilizes the full power of the Epoq Zero CRM, integrating it deeply into their daily workflows. This long-term partnership stands as a prime example of how Epoq Zero can scale with a client, moving from early-stage support to maintaining a massive, multi-faceted digital ecosystem.
